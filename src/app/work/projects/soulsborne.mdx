## Overview

The Soulsborne Pitch Deck application is designed to help students practice and enhance their pitching skills. By providing a structured environment for creating and presenting pitch decks, the app aims to help students gain confidence and become better communicators.

## Key Features

- **Pitch Deck Builder**: Developed a tool for students to create and customize pitch decks with templates and guided prompts.
- **Real-time Feedback**: Integrated a feedback system where users can receive ratings and suggestions on their pitch presentations from peers and mentors.
- **Analytics and Progress Tracking**: Created a feature to track users' progress and improvement over time, with detailed analytics of their pitch scores.
- **Collaborative Features**: Enabled users to collaborate with others on pitch decks, making it ideal for group projects and team-based pitching.
- **Smart Suggestions**: Added AI-powered suggestions for improving pitch content based on successful pitch patterns.
- **Google Login**: Added Google login to simplify login process.

## Technologies Used

- **Next.js**: For building the frontend and ensuring fast server-side rendering.
- **MongoDB**: For storing pitch deck data and user feedback.
- **Cloudinary**: For handling media uploads in pitch decks.
- **AI Algorithms**: For providing smart pitch suggestions based on past data and user input.
- **Clerk**: For google signup and login

## Challenges and Learnings

One challenge was designing an intuitive and easy-to-use interface for students who may not have experience creating pitch decks. This was overcome by providing user-friendly templates and step-by-step guidance. Additionally, ensuring real-time feedback was smoothly integrated was a key learning experience.

## Outcome

The Soulsborne Pitch Deck application has empowered students to practice their pitching skills, receiving valuable feedback and improving their abilities. It has become an essential tool for students preparing for competitions and real-world presentations.
